About Company


During the 2017–18 school year, 80 percent of public schools recorded that one or more incidents of violence, theft or other crimes had taken place, amounting to 1.4 million incidents. This translates to a rate of 29 incidents per 1,000 students enrolled in 2017– 18. During the same school year, 47 percent of schools reported one or more incidents of violence, theft, or other crimes to the police, amounting to 422,800 incidents, or 9 incidents per 1,000 students enrolled.


Increasing domestic violence in schools can be minimized if there is a way to call out for help at that instant of happening. So here is a proposal, how about a device that can call out for help or alert a specific person with a single press of a button. this product is specifically designed for students who go to college and school. In most educational institutions mobile phones are prohibited eventually they don’t have any mode of contact with their parents or guardians giving them a gadget specifically to call or intimate there will be a chance to lose them. rather than giving a gadget, it will be much more efficient if we have a design to combine a gadget which can call, with gadget students already carry.so I integrate a gadget that can call and carry their id card. It's further integrated with a QR code for money transactions and a flashlight. Id-phone can only make calls, can’t receive one which is an added advantage, which avoids calls inside the campus in between the id-phone holders, id phone can’t call another id-phone it can only call a mobile phone or a landline. There is an option to attach a tag, so students can place their id’s on the adjustable clamp and swing along their necks.

Specifications


PRIME BUTTONS: ID-PHONE has a sum of 5 prime buttons in which every button calls a specific number when pressed. these buttons are marked with shapes so people who don’t know about numbering systems can also use them, as it’s designed for children who go to schools, shapes are specifically used so that even a 3yr old can use them, scientific research has proven by three years of age, a child should be able to identify some basic shapes. It is designed with braille keys so a blind can use it.


CALL AND DECLINE BUTTON: Green button: after pressing the prime button press the green button to call every pressing you will hear a beep which adds up the clarity. Red button: After the call, press the red button to decline.
